Our Story

From seed to bottle —
nothing but purity

At Aarvaya, we carry forward centuries-old traditions of cold-pressing and stone-grinding. Every drop of oil and every grain of flour that leaves our mill is a promise of unadulterated goodness — no chemicals, no preservatives, just honest nourishment from the earth.

Why Aarvaya

The Aarvaya difference

🌿
Cold-Pressed Process

Extracted at low temperatures, preserving all natural nutrients, antioxidants and that authentic aroma you can taste.

🪨
Stone-Ground Tradition

Flours ground on traditional stone chakki mills — slow, careful, retaining the bran, germ and full nutritional value.

🌾
Farm-Sourced Ingredients

We work directly with trusted local farmers to source the finest coconuts, sesame, groundnuts and grains.
